about us

our story?

The owner first thought that live selling would be a good business since it was very popular at the time. So, on May 4, 2021, she started an ukay-ukay business, but she gave up quickly because it wasn't successful. Since her hobby was painting nails, she decided to turn that into a business.She began offering nail painting services in June and started taking clients in September. She takes on clients like students who want to work part-time and have no experience because she prefers to teach those who are eager to learn about nail polish application.

Her nail business was successful, so she decided to open her own caf√© called "Crystal Polish." It originally started as a nail salon, but one day, the owner noticed that her employees arrived every morning with coffee in hand. She thought, "Why not add a coffee shop to the nail salon?" So she did, and this expansion made her salon even bigger and attracted more customers.
